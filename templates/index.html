What are we dealing with?

The Global Burden of Disease project reveals skin diseases as the 4th leading cause of nonfatal disease worldwide. These conditions often lead to severe systemic illnesses like HIV and neglected tropical diseases. However, providing effective dermatological care in under-served regions is challenging due to inadequate diagnostic tools, poor infrastructure, and scarcity of trained physicians.

How do we do it?

Derma is a cross-platform web application designed to aid in the examination and detection of dermatological diseases. It empowers users to capture images of affected areas on a patient's skin, which are then analyzed by a deep learning model integrated into the application's backend. This model delivers highly accurate results, promptly identifying the skin condition. Subsequently, the user receives the diagnosis and has the opportunity to engage with a specialized chatbot to discuss the disease, treatment options, and next steps.
