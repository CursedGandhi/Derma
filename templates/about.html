The general validity of the model's predictions is gauged by its Accuracy. The goal is to maximize it. It is calculated by taking the ratio of accurately predicted instances to all instances.

Loss is the measure of difference between predicted values and actual values. The goal of training is to minimize the loss value. For this model, we are using the categorical cross-entropy loss function which is used for classification problems.
